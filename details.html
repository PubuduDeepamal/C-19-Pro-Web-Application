Four Covid vaccines approved for use in Sri Lanka

Pfizer covid vaccine

The Pfizer vaccine is a vaccine that aims to protect against COVID-19.

The Food and Drug Administration granted full approval to
Pfizer-BioNTech's coronavirus vaccine for people 16 and up.

Sri Lanka approves Pfizer vaccine for emergency use.

Russia's Sputnik

The Sputnik V vaccine is a vaccine that aims to protect against COVID-19.

It is the world's first registered combination vector vaccine for the prevention of COVID-19.

Sri Lanka approves Sputnik vaccine for emergency use.

China's Sinopharm

The Sinopharm vaccine is a vaccine that aims to protect against COVID-19.

Sri Lanka approves China's Sinopharm vaccine for emergency use.

Moderna vaccine

The Moderna vaccine is a vaccine that aims to protect against COVID-19.

It is recommended for people aged 18 years and older.

Sri Lanka approves Moderna vaccine for emergency use.
